About Nasagh

Nasagh creates timeless Arabian fragrances that honor tradition and craft.

Nasagh (نسغ) refers to the noble sap that flows within the heart of trees. Our brand is inspired by this pure essence, just as oud is drawn from the core of fragrant wood.

We specialize in premium Arabian perfumes, crafted with deep respect for heritage and an eye for contemporary luxury design. Every bottle carries a story of the Middle East—its deserts, majlis gatherings, and timeless traditions.

Our Values

  • Authenticity – using high-quality oils and natural extracts.
  • Transparency – clear information about ingredients and sourcing.
  • Elegance – refined visual design, packaging, and presentation.
  • Cultural Pride – celebrating Arabian heritage in every scent.
